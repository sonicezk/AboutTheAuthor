About the Author

Ethan Knapp

Ethan Knapp is a student at West Lutheran High School in Plymouth, Minnesota. Currently in his Junior year, he dedicates his time to a mixture of school and extracurricular activities. He participates in band, is Vice President of the National Honor's Society for West Lutheran High School, is a devout member of Mock Trial and avid trap shooter, and participates in pep band for school events. He hopes to go to the University of Minnesota in Twin Cities for a degree in computer science when he gets older, and wishes to be a software engineer or video game programmer when he graduates.
